## 4. Slug Generation

When a user saves a new Business Profile:

Automatically generate a slug from the Business Name.

Example:

Business Name:

ABC Dental Clinic

Slug:

abc-dental-clinic

Rules:

- Lowercase
- Replace spaces with hyphens
- Remove special characters
- Collapse multiple hyphens
- Trim leading/trailing hyphens

If the slug already exists:

Append:

-2

Then:

-3

Etc.

Examples:

abc-dental-clinic

abc-dental-clinic-2

abc-dental-clinic-3

The slug generation should be deterministic and reliable.
