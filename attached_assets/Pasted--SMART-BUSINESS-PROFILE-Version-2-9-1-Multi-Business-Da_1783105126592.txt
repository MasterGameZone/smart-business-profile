## Standard Instructions

Before making any changes:

## 1. Objective

Upgrade the Business Dashboard to support multiple business profiles owned by a single authenticated user.

This replaces the previous assumption that each user owns only one business profile.

---

## 2. Existing Context

The project already includes:

- Authentication
- Protected Routes
- Business Ownership
- Business CRUD
- Public Business Profiles
- Business Dashboard

Continue working inside the existing project.

Do not restructure the application.

---

## 3. Dashboard Retrieval

Replace the current single-profile query.

Instead of expecting exactly one profile:

Retrieve all business profiles belonging to the authenticated user.

Create or update a reusable function:

getBusinessProfilesByOwner(ownerId)

Requirements:

- Query business_profiles
- Filter using owner_id
- Order by created_at descending
- Return an array of Business Profiles

Do NOT use .single().

---

## 4. Dashboard Layout

Replace the "My Business" section with:

"My Businesses"

Display every owned business as a card.

Each card should display:

- Business Logo (or placeholder)
- Business Name
- Business Category
- Slug
- Created Date
- Last Updated Date

---

## 5. Business Actions
